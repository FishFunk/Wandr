Travel Guru

The folks at Travel Guru want to mutual friend introductions a reality again and on a global scale! By leveraging 1st and 2nd degree connections, your network grows exponentially. The average Facebook user has approximately 380 friends online. This means, your network potential is 144,000 people! A wide reaching network has many uses. Chances are, a network this large includes a diverse group of people that live in all different places of the world. These are people with at least one common friend of yours that may be willing to host you as a traveler, meet up with you for drinks, provide you local tips and recommendations, or simply just be a contact point in case of an emergency. Having a local contact and familiar face can greatly enhance your travel or moving experience and likely lead to a renewed or budding friendship! Our app unlocks the potential of these connections making it simple and fun.
