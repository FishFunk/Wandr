Wandr

Wandr was created by a team that believes in the power of bringing people together, wandering our world, and having new experiences.

How you ask? We leverage your 1st and 2nd degree social connections in order to increase your network potential! Let's say you have 250 Facebook friends and each of them have 250 friends. This means, your network potential is roughly 62,500 people! A network this vast likely includes a diverse group of people that live in all different areas of the world which you can visually see on your connection map.

Whether you're looking to make a new local friend, host travellers or stay with locals abroad, get local tips, or share a coffee and conversation with a friendly face, Wandr can help you do so with one simple, fun, safe, and interactive experience!
